================================================================================
⚙️  QUICK SETUP
================================================================================

1. Add email credentials to .env.local:
   EMAIL_USER=your-email@gmail.com
   EMAIL_PASS=app-specific-password

2. Test by placing an order (should receive confirmation email)

3. Setup cron job (optional):
   - Use Upstash, EasyCron, or Vercel
   - Point to: /api/cron/email-triggers?token=CRON_SECRET
